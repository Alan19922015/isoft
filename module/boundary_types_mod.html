integer, public, parameter:: free_boundary =-1

Indicates that the solution may take any value at the boundary.
integer, public, parameter:: dirichlet =0

Indicates that the value of the solution at this boundary is prescribed.
integer, public, parameter:: neumann =1

Indicates that the first derivative of the solution at this boundary is prescribed.
integer, public, parameter:: cauchy =5

Indicates that the value of the solution and its first derivative are both prescribed at this boundary.
integer, public, parameter:: robin =6

Indicates that the linear combination of the solution's value and first derivative is prescribed at this boundary.
